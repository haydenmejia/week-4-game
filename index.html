Crystal Collector

INSTRUCTIONS

1. You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

2. There are four crystals below. By clicking on a button you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

3. You win the game by matching your total score to a random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

4. The value of each crytal is hidden from you until you click on the button.

5. Each time when the game starts, the game will change the value of each crystal.
